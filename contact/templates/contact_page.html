In an emergency
call 999

An emergency is if someone's life is in danger or someone is committing a crime.

If you landlord (or anyone else) has turned up at your home without a court order and bailiffs, this is an emergency and you should call 999 and ask for the police.

The Peterborough Tenants Union is run by volunteers, so it can take us some time to reply - however we will prioritise urgent cases and support requests.
